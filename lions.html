LIONS

The lion(Panthera leo) is a large felid of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a muscular, deep-chested body, short, round ears and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail. It is sexually dimorphic, adult male lions are longer than females and have a prominent mane. It is a social species, forming groups called prides. A lion's pride consists of a few adult males, related females, and cubs. Groups of female lions usually hunt together, although some lions scavenge when opportunities occur and have been known to hunt humans, the species typically does not.
