MONKEYS

Monkey is a common name theat may refer to most simian mammals of infraorder simiiformes. Traditionally, all animals in the group now known as simians are counted as monkeys excepts the apes, in the broader sense based on claudistics, apes may also be included, making the terms monkeys and smians synonyms in egard of their scope. Monkeys are divided into families of new wold monkeys and old world monkeys.

Many monkeys species are tree-dwelling, although there are species that live primarily on the ground, such as baboons. Most species are mainly active during the day. Monkeys are generally considered to be intelligent, especially the old world monkeys.
